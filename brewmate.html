BrewMate

a coffee brewing app

The Idea

An app that allows users full control over their coffee brews from the very beginning, like selecting brew types and ratios - through to the end, like describing the flavour profile and sharing with friends.

Visually it was really important to me that this app is easy on early morning eyes!

Learning from the competition

Seeing what the competition was doing so I could establish what was being done well, what could be improved upon and what is missing was an important early step.

User Flows

I established some primary user flows to guide what screens to focus on designing first and give a path to follow throughout those screens.
