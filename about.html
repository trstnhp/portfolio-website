Hi, I'm Tristan

Thanks for dropping by.

I’m a UI Designer, on the beginning of my journey into front-end web development. But I’ve not always been like this. My first job was at the age of 15 as a pot washer in a hotel kitchen. From this I learned to be humble, the meaning of hard work, and most importantly that I hate washing up. So I learned to cook. By 21 I was running my own kitchen. I changed careers and got my first retail job at Apple as a part-time sales member. From this I learned that I love working in tech, with big multi-disciplined teams and I learned the impact of great design. Over 8 years, and 8 promotions I became a Merchandising Manager. So now I’m here. A UI Designer - with an eye for great design, passion, a great work ethic and a team-player.
