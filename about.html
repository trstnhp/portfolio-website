about me

My Story

I’ve loved technology for as long as I can remember - I was lucky enough to grow up with a Macintosh as our family computer and from about the age of about 6 I was the go to person for my parents if they needed any help using it. I loved it. That Mac was the first computer I ever used to design anything - my brother and I, with help from our Dad designed a dashboard for a Space Ship (a few colourful squares and circles) that we attached to our beds and used to fight off any attacking monsters at night. I will always remember the feeling of coming up with ideas for the design, implementing them and printing out our finished dashboards. Whenever I design anything now - I always feel myself going back to that moment.

I’ve been lucky enough to work in tech for the last 8 years, for the very company that made that computer I made my very first design on in fact! At Apple I started in Retail as a sales specialist when I was 21 and worked my way up through sales, and tech support to now be a Merchandising Manager, with that comes incredible passion for how I can positively impact the customer experience in our stores by taking them on a personal journey and telling stories with our products. All while upholding Apple’s incredible high level of standards and very defined merchandising design systems.
